###url重写一例###

* &lt;Directory "D:/ApacheGroup/www"&gt;
* RewriteEngine on
* RewriteCond %{REQUEST_FILENAME} !-f
* RewriteRule ^([a-zA-Z]+)/index.html$  index.php?controller=$1 [L]
* RewriteRule ^([a-zA-Z]+)/([a-zA-Z]+).html$  index.php?controller=$1&action=$2 [L]

* &lt;/Directory&gt;
